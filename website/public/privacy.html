PRIVACY

Your privacy

MatchIntel only collects the information needed to run accounts, licenses, free trials and support the service.

Discord sign-in

When you claim a free trial, Discord gives MatchIntel your Discord user ID and basic profile name. MatchIntel does not request access to your messages, servers or friends.

Licensing

MatchIntel uses a protected device identifier when a key is activated. This helps enforce license limits and prevents repeated free-trial abuse. The public website does not ask you to enter a device ID.

What may be stored

  • Your Discord user ID and display name
  • Your license status and expiration date
  • A protected device identifier used for activation
  • Basic security and support records

Service providers

Discord handles sign-in, and Railway hosts the MatchIntel website, backend and database.

Questions

Use the support section to contact MatchIntel about privacy or account data.
