FAQ

What players actually want to know.

What can I see during a live tournament match?

You can see the full lobby, every team, player PR and earnings, who is alive or eliminated, team eliminations, placement, storm time, surge and your own team in one dashboard.

How do I load my match into MatchIntel?

Paste the tournament session ID into MatchIntel. The dashboard then loads the players and teams from that session and keeps the match updated.

How often does the lobby update?

MatchIntel refreshes the live match data automatically. You can change the refresh speed in settings, with five seconds as the normal default.

How do the Storm Sickness and surge trackers help?

The storm tracker shows how long you have spent in storm before Storm Sickness becomes a problem. The surge tracker shows whether your team is above or below the current surge line.

Can I quickly find the strongest teams?

Yes. Sort the lobby by combined PR, highest individual PR, earnings, eliminations, placement or current status to find the teams that matter fastest.

Can I keep my own team easy to find?

Yes. Enter your Fortnite name to highlight your team, pin it and keep your placement, eliminations and points visible while the lobby changes.

Which matches and team modes are supported?

MatchIntel is built for supported Fortnite tournament sessions in Solos, Duos, Trios and Squads. Normal pubs and Ranked matches are not currently supported.

Is MatchIntel designed to be safe for competitive Fortnite?

MatchIntel is designed not to modify game files, access game memory or interact with the Fortnite process. It uses a screenshot at the beginning of the match to identify the session. See the Competitive Safety section for the full explanation and Epic Support conversation.

Can I review my games after the tournament?

Yes. MatchIntel keeps tournament history with placement, eliminations, points, lobby strength, averages, best games and other useful performance stats.

What is included in the free trial?

The two-day trial includes the full MatchIntel dashboard, so you can use the real features before deciding whether to buy lifetime access.

What do I need to run MatchIntel?

You need a Windows PC with Overwolf, an internet connection and a supported tournament session ID. MatchIntel also includes performance settings for lighter usage.

Where do I get help if something is not working?

Join the MatchIntel Discord for installation help, key support, bug reports and updates.
